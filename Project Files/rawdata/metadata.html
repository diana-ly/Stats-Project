Title: Salary information for NBA players of the 2014-2015 season.
Dataset ID: raw_salaries.csv
Latest Update: December 9, 2015
Abstract: The purpose of this dataset is to gather the salary information for each player in the NBA 2014-2015 season. We will then use the player's name and and their salary to merge it with another dataset consisting of the player statistics. After this, we will be able to do an analysis on the final dataset to answer our research question (Do the skills of player relate to their salary?)
Size: 410 entries, excluding the header
Storage mode: Comma delimited file
Header information: There is one main header consisting of RK, NAME, TEAM, and SALARY that divides every ten (10) entries.


Variable Name Variable Description Units Storage Type
RK Ranking of players according to their salary. n/a Numeric
Name First and last name of player followed by their position in their team. n/a Character
Team Name of corresponding player's team. n/a Character
Salary Player's salary during the NBA season of 2014-2015. USD Numeric

Title: United States Institution Information as of 2013
Dataset ID: US_Colleges.csv (in /rawdata)
Latest Update: December 9, 2015
Abstract: The purpose of this dataset is to get the longitude and latitude coordinates of all licensed institutions in the United States as of 2013. We will use the names of these colleges and universities along with the coordinates to merge it with the roster dataset. This dataset will be used to show which colleges and universities (East vs West) that produced the most NBA players of the 2014-2015 season.
Size: 7764 entries, excluding the header
Storage mode: Comma delimited file
Header information: There is one main header consisting of ID, institution name, year, Address, City, State, Zip code, Longitude, Latitude, HD2013.Institution name alias.

Latitude Longitude of the institution. n/a Numeric
HD2013.Institution.name.alias Alieses of the institution's name. NA values are coded as blanks. n/a Character

Title: Player statistics of all NBA players of the 2014-2015 season
Dataset ID: [Team].csv ([Team] is replaced by the team mascot or its abbreviation: Raptors, Cavaliers, Hawks, TB, Warriors, Rockets, Celtics, Nets, 76ers, Knicks, Bulls, Bucks, Pacers, Pistons, Wizards, Heat, Hornets, Magic, Thunder, Jazz, Nuggets, Timberwolves, Clippers, Suns, Kings, Lakers, Spurs, Griz, Mavs, Pelicans)
Latest Update: December 9, 2015
Abstract: The purpose of this dataset is to gather all of the player statistics of all the players of the 2014-2015 NBA season. We will use this dataset to merge with the salary dataset in order to answer our research question (Do the skills of player relate to their salary?). We will analyze the information provided for point guard, center, small forward, power forward, and shooting guard versus salary.
Size: 7764 entries, excluding the header
Storage mode: Comma delimited file
Header information: There is one main header consisting of Rk, Player, Age, G, GS, MP, FG, FGA, FG%, 3P, 3PA, 3P%, 2P, 2PA, 2P%, eFG%, FT, FTA, FT%, ORB, DRB, TRB, AST, STL, BLK, TOV, PF, PTS.
