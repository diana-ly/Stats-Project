Title: Salary information for NBA players of the 2014-2015 season.
Dataset ID: raw_salaries.csv
Latest Update: December 9, 2015
Abstract: The purpose of this dataset is to gather the salary information for each player in the NBA 2014-2015 season. We will then use the player's name and and their salary to merge it with another dataset consisting of the player statistics. After this, we will be able to do an analysis on the final dataset to answer our research question (Do the skills of player relate to their salary?)
Size: 410 entries, excluding the header
Storage mode: Comma delimited file
Header information: There is one main header consisting of RK, NAME, TEAM, and SALARY that divides every ten (10) entries.

Title: United States Institution Information as of 2013
Dataset ID: US_Colleges.csv (in /rawdata)
Latest Update: December 9, 2015
Abstract: The purpose of this dataset is to get the longitude and latitude coordinates of all licensed institutions in the United States as of 2013. We will use the names of these colleges and universities along with the coordinates to merge it with the roster dataset. This dataset will be used to show which colleges and universities (East vs West) that produced the most NBA players of the 2014-2015 season.
